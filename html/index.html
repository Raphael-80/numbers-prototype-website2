About Us

Welcome to Numbers Bukateria

A culinary haven that beckons patrons with its elegant ambiance and tantalizing fare. Nestled in Redemmers University, its environment creates an inviting atmosphere for diners seeking an unforgettable experience. Numbers boasts an electric menu that showcases a symphony of flavours from around Nigeria.

Alongside its delectable cuisine, the restaurant offers a plethora of recreational activities to delight guests of all ages. Whether it's a spirited game of football, a fast-paced match of badminton or table tennis, a selection of board games with an array of events from open mic nights and lively parties. There's never a dull moments at Numbers.
